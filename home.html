About Jocole.

Who We Are

Jocole. means "Jewelry" in latin creating handcrafted jewelry in Canada. We believe that luxury should be affordable and accessible without compromosing quality, so we strive to deliver well-made, longlasting, and classic jewelry to last you a lifetime.

Who We Are Made For

We are made for the people that live life in a slow fashion mind-set. Investing in pieces for the quality and classic style, and knowing that these pieces accentuate the beauty of the owner. More importantly, we are inspired you; a real, smart, and confident individual.

Why Choose Jocole

We pride ourself of our jewelry making process, only working with the top-jewelers in Canada with the same ethical principles us. While we strive to ensure a high-quality product for you, we mke sure that you never pay more than what you should.
